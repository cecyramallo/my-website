ABOUT ME

Hello! I am Cecilia Ramallo. I am Chilean/Bolivian/Peruvian and I love to travel, so I consider myself as a world citizen. I studied Front-End Development at Laboratoria Chile, a programming bootcamp for women where I learned different tech and soft skills in only six months. Previously, I had 10 years of experience working as a historian, classical guitarist, musicologist and language teacher. I am very good at organizing, planning, managing time and budgets, working as part of a team and as a leader, writing and speaking in English, French and Spanish. I aim at using all my studies and work experience to keep growing in the tech industry. My objectives are to become a Full Stack Developer and a Product Manager, and later to create my own startup.
